# feel cards scenario-specific response options
winded.vertigo · feel cards · draft 24 april 2026

**structure:** each scenario has 6 response options, 2 per intelligence.
players are dealt 4 from the pool of 6 each round, guaranteeing they always see at least one from each intelligence type.

**ci responses** focus on the problem, the idea, the reframe, the options
**ei responses** focus on the internal emotional experience and what to do with it
**collab responses** focus on the group, the dynamic, the other people in the room

archetype assignment follows the intelligence type:
ci → branching · horizon · spiral · doorway
ei → wave · pool · fracture · echo
collab → threads · broken_circle · column · mountain

---

## creative intelligence · scenarios 1–15

---

**1 · B2B framing not holding, deadline in one hour**
[ci] branching: i sketch two alternative framings quickly, pick the stronger one and rebuild in the time i have
[ci] spiral: i keep reworking the same section, convinced the right version is close, just not there yet
[ei] wave: i'm flooded by the deadline and decide to send what i have with a clear note on what's unresolved
[ei] pool: i step away from the model for ten minutes and come back with different eyes
[collab] column: i finish the current version, flag the weak point explicitly and deliver on time
[collab] mountain: i accept this version, document my assumptions clearly and hand it over

[collab] threads: i watch who in the room responds to the idea and what that tells me about the group's appetite for risk

## notes

**why 2+2+2:** the goal is that when three players compare their picks, each person's choice is legible as a response rooted in one of the three intelligences. the debrief question becomes not just "why did you pick this" but "which intelligence was driving you in this moment."

**archetype-to-intelligence mapping:**
ci → branching · horizon · spiral · doorway
ei → wave · pool · fracture · echo
collab → threads · broken_circle · column · mountain

**a few archetypes appear outside their primary cluster** (doorway in collab scenarios, echo in collab scenarios, pool in collab scenarios) when the label carries the right intelligence flavor. the label is what matters; the archetype is the visual identity.

**dealing from the pool:** with 6 options per scenario (2 per intelligence), dealing 4 means each player will always see at least 2 of the 3 intelligences represented in their hand. across a trio, the full 2+2+2 is almost always visible.
